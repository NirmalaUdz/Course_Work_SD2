Our Projects done by
Projects Location Description Impact Project Status
Clean Water for All Kenya Installed solar-powered water pumps in rural communities to provide clean drinking water. Provided clean water to over 10,000 people, reducing waterborne diseases and improving health.
Rainwater Harvesting India Implemented rainwater harvesting systems in drought-prone areas to capture and store rainwater. Increased water availability for agriculture and household use, reducing dependency on groundwater.
EcoSan Toilets Uganda Built eco-friendly sanitation facilities that convert human waste into compost for agriculture. Improved sanitation for 5,000 households and promoted sustainable farming practices.
Water Credit Initiative Bangladesh Provided microloans for low-income families to build water and sanitation facilities. Enabled 50,000 families to access clean water and improved sanitation, enhancing quality of life.
